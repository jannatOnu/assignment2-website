Body mass index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight that applies to adult men and women. View the BMI tables:

BMI Calculator Chart

BMI Weight Status
Below 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Healthy
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight

Calculate your BMI

BMI is calculated by dividing your weight in kilograms by your height in metres squared. Enter your measurements and click 'calculate' for your body mass index.
